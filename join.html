Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim my listing?
Find your business on the map, click "Claim This Listing," fill out the form, and complete payment. We'll verify your information and activate your listing within 24 hours.

No problem! When you sign up, we'll create a new listing for you. Just provide your business details during the claim process.
What's the SEO backlink benefit?
Your verified profile includes a dofollow link to your website. This tells Google your site is legitimate, helping your own website rank higher in search results.

How do profile view stats work?
We track how many people view your profile each month. Verified members can see their stats on their profile page. Premium members get a monthly email report.

Currently, the verified badge indicates you've claimed and paid for your listing. We're working on adding FAA certification verification as an additional trust signal.
